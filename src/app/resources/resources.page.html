The Dubai Big 4

Experience the iconic landmarks that define this remarkable city

1

Burj Khalifa

At 828 meters, the world's tallest building offers breathtaking views from its observation decks on floors 124, 125, and 148. Visit at sunset for the most spectacular experience as the city transforms from day to night.

Book tickets online in advance to skip the queues
2

Dubai Mall

More than just shopping—this mega-mall features over 1,200 stores, the Dubai Aquarium, an Olympic-sized ice rink, and the mesmerizing Dubai Fountain show outside.

Fountain shows run every 30 minutes from 6 PM
3

The Palm Jumeirah

This engineering marvel is visible from space—a man-made archipelago shaped like a palm tree. Home to luxury resorts and the famous Atlantis resort.

Visit The View at The Palm for 360° panoramic views
4

Al Fahidi Historical District

Step back in time in Old Dubai's preserved heritage site. Wander through narrow lanes, visit traditional wind-tower houses, art galleries, and the Dubai Museum.

Best visited in early morning or late afternoon
