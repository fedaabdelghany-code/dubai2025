At 828 meters, the world's tallest building offers breathtaking views from its observation decks on floors 124, 125, and 148. Visit at sunset for the most spectacular experience as the city transforms from day to night.

Book tickets online in advance to skip the queues

Getting Around Dubai

Navigate the city with ease

Dubai Metro

Best for: Quick, affordable travel along main routes
Lines: Red & Green Lines
Cost: AED 3-8.50 per journey
Tip: Get a Nol Card at any station for seamless travel.

Water Taxi & Abra

Best for: Creek crossings and scenic rides
Traditional Abra: AED 1 per crossing
Tip: The traditional abra across Dubai Creek is an authentic local experience.

Taxis & Ride-Hailing

Best for: Door-to-door convenience
Apps: Careem, Uber widely available
Tip: Careem often offers better rates during peak hours.

Public Buses

Best for: Budget-friendly city exploration
Cost: AED 3-7.50 per journey
Tip: All buses are air-conditioned. Use Google Maps for routes.

Dubai Tram & Walking

Best for: Marina, JBR, and waterfront areas
Cost: AED 3-6.50
Tip: Walking is pleasant Nov-Mar. Summer can exceed 40¬∞C.
